Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Privacy is a core principle at Anka TMS. Our apps are designed to work without collecting, storing, or transmitting your personal data.

Our Approach

Anka TMS builds offline-first, privacy-first applications. We do not operate servers that store user data. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not sell or share data with third parties.

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • No crash reporting or diagnostics
  • No device identifiers or fingerprinting
  • No advertising data

iCloud Sync

Some of our apps offer optional iCloud sync via Apple CloudKit. When enabled, your data is stored in your private iCloud account and is:

  • Only accessible to devices signed in with your Apple ID
  • Not readable by Anka TMS or any third party
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policy and iCloud terms

This Website

This website (ankatms.com) is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts. The only external resource loaded is Google Fonts.

Children's Privacy

Our apps designed for family use do not collect any data from children. All data is managed locally by the parent on the device.
