Anka TMS /Sparkle/Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Sparkle is designed with privacy at its core. All your data stays on your device. We collect nothing, track nothing, and share nothing.

Data Collection

Sparkle does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All information you enter — kids' names, tasks, stars, rewards — is stored locally on your device.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your private iCloud account (Apple CloudKit). This data is:

  • Only accessible to devices signed in with your Apple ID
  • Not readable by Anka TMS or any third party
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policy and iCloud terms

iCloud sync is optional and can be disabled at any time in Settings.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services. There are no third-party SDKs in Sparkle.

Third-Party Services

Sparkle does not integrate with any third-party services that would have access to your data. The only external service is Apple's iCloud if you choose to enable sync.

Children's Privacy

Sparkle is designed for families with kids roughly aged 4–12. A parent or guardian operates the app and enters any information about their child (name, optional birthday, avatar). We do not collect any data from children, and no data ever reaches Anka TMS servers. Parent-controlled features include a PIN-protected section for task and reward management.

Deleting Your Data

You control all data at all times. To remove it:

  • Delete a single kid profile from within the app — cascades to all their tasks, rewards, and history
  • Disable iCloud sync in Settings to stop cross-device syncing (existing data remains until you delete it)
  • Uninstall the app — removes local data from your device. By Apple's design, iCloud-synced data is not automatically deleted, so other signed-in devices keep their copy. To also remove the iCloud copy, go to iOS Settings → your Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Sparkle → Delete Data

Because no data leaves your devices, there is nothing to request from Anka TMS.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, reach out via our support page or email support@ankatms.com.
